# Privacy Policy Last updated: March 2026 ## Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how the app Busta Paga handles information. The app is designed to help users track working hours and estimate payroll calculations. Your privacy is important to us. ## Data Collection Busta Paga does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered in the app (such as working hours, salary estimates, or notes) is stored locally on your device. ## Analytics This app does not use analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks. ## Third-Party Services The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. ## Data Storage All data is stored locally using Apple's system frameworks. If backup features are used, data may be stored in locations chosen by the user (such as local files or personal cloud storage). ## Children's Privacy This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer. Developer: Busta Paga App
